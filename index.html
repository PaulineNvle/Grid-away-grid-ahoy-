Shiba Inu

The Shiba Inu is a lively and very intelligent dog. Small but well proportioned, it has a strong personality. Despite its independent character, it is very faithful and protective. It is not the most cuddly of dogs, but has many qualities that will delight young and old. He is sincere and faithful in his approach as well as in his approach. His health and maintenance are assets because they do not require much time.

Maltese

The Maltese Bichon is a small, calm and very intelligent dog with a narrow and long trunk, with a long white and silky hair. It is a very elegant little dog with a proud and distinguished head. The Maltese Bichon is an adorable companion dog with a very fine coat that should be taken care of with the greatest delicacy. The maintenance of this canine is special. For the rest, its education and nutrition are rather easy. He is a very pleasant dog, attached to his master.

Australian Shepherd

The Australian Shepherd is an affectionate dog that does not lack character. A dog built for activity and work, the Australian Shepherd is equipped with all the physical and mental skills to fulfill various missions. Medium-sized, agile and intelligent, it fits perfectly into family life and adapts to a variety of living environments, provided you don’t let it get bored. In recent years, this breed has enjoyed great success in the hearts of the French.

Cocker Spaniel

The English Cocker Spaniel is a truly popular breed. Its character appeals to many families. Despite its long hair, its maintenance remains reasonable since only a daily brushing is mandatory. He is intelligent, docile, gentle, pleasant to live and constantly seeks to satisfy his master. In addition, he will accompany her in each of her sports outings.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a robust, athletic and enduring dog. It has a very harmonious body and is built for physical effort. For 10 years, he has reigned supreme on the 1st place of the podium of the French' favorite dogs. The German Shepherd is a complete dog, tireless, docile, vigilant and very attached to its owner.

Dachshund

The Dachshund is a low-legged dog with an elongated body. Despite its short limbs, the Dachshund is remarkably lively and agile. There are 3 different sizes (standard, dwarf and Kaninchen) and 3 varieties of hair: short, hard and long.

Weimaraner

The Braque de Weimar is a German-made dog of medium to large size. It has an athletic body, both slender and muscular. Ideal companion for families, it is also the ultimate hunting dog.

Corgi

The Welsh Corgi Pembroke is a low-legged dog, robust and rather compact. This fox-like dog is lighter and smaller than its close cousin, the Welsh Corgi Cardigan.
